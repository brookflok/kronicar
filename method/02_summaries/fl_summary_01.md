# Sažetak FL 01 — Katalog 20 fantastičnih lokacija

Kompaktan katalog (pokriva oba location batcha, pp 6-87). Za svaku lokaciju: koncept, **Location Aspects** (tagovi iz knjige) i **Potential Inhabitants**. Mape namjerno ne postoje. Sve je za reskin u bilo koji setting (uključujući Sharn). Imena u originalu.

1. **The Ziggurats of the Doom Priests** (str. 5) — zabranjene zigurate zakopane u džungli, hram smrti i žrtvovanja. *Aspects:* isolated old ruin, hidden temple, haunted catacombs, forbidden towers. *Inhabitants:* undead, humanoids, monstrous humanoids, unintelligent monsters, constructs.
2. **The Structure in the Ice** (str. 9) — drevna vanzemaljska/vanplanarna građevina otkrivena u ledu (više mogućih porijekla). *Aspects:* uncovered, unexplored, otherworldly, ancient, arctic, extraterrestrial, extraplanar. *Inhabitants:* outsiders, demons, devils, angels, aberrations, aliens, constructs, magical beings.
3. **Pyramid of the Night King** (str. 14) — grobnica-hram imperatorske dinastije stare desetine hiljada godina, puna zamki. *Aspects:* tomb, temple, undiscovered, traps, magical. *Inhabitants:* undead, constructs, demons, outsiders, desert nomads.
4. **The Undercity** (str. 19) — bezakoni podzemni grad šverca i kriminala. *Aspects:* lawless, active, smuggling, assassins, pirates, slavery, corruption, contraband, kidnapping. *Inhabitants:* humanoids, monstrous humanoids, cultists, thieves. (Idealno za reskin u Khyber's Gate / Lower Dura.)
5. **The Dark Abbey** (str. 22) — opatija zla, centar zloće, tama vrtloži nebom. *Aspects:* forbidden, evil, temple, sacrifice. *Inhabitants:* cultists, monstrous humanoids, undead, demons, vampires.
6. **The Elven City of Moonwillow** (str. 26) — drevni elfski grad star deset hiljada godina (kamenorez), sad u ruševinama. *Aspects:* forest, ancient ruins, mountains, grove. *Inhabitants:* monstrous humanoids, devils, demons, undead. (Reskin: Shae Lias lore / izgubljena enklava.)
7. **The Lich's Sanctuary** (str. 30) — keep bez vrata, dostupan samo magijom (cursed tome / teleportation scroll). *Aspects:* astral, ethereal, extradimensional, planar, cursed tome, unknown teleportation scroll. *Inhabitants:* constructs, demons, devils, undead, magical traps. (Reskin: Mausoleum of Gath / City of the Dead.)
8. **The Fallen Palace of the Celestials** (str. 34) — palata palih nebesnika, "ranjena svijest". *Aspects:* mountainous, secluded, celestial, otherworldly. *Inhabitants:* celestials, devils, demons, mutants, outsiders, dungeon delvers. (Reskin: radiant idols / Syrania veza.)
9. **The Dungeon of Fire** (str. 38) — vulkanski podzemni kompleks. *Aspects:* mountainous, secluded, volcanic, otherworldly. *Inhabitants:* dark dwarves, dark elves, goblinoids, celestials, demons, outsiders, dragons. (Reskin: the Cogs / lava peći.)
10. **The Red Keep** (str. 42) — tvrđava nad žilama "zlog crvenog gvožđa" koje se obnavlja; koncept: zaboravljeni bog/biće zatočeno milenijima u dubokom vaultu. *Aspects:* veins of evil red iron, dwarven ruins, bloody battlegrounds, isolated mountains, vertical city architecture, abandoned mines, ancient hidden chambers. *Inhabitants:* demons, devils, fallen celestials, dark elves, goblinoids, undead, evil humanoids, crazed maniacs, evil cultists, aberrations. ("Vertical city architecture" — direktno korisno za Sharn.)
11. **The Dam of Kings** (str. 46) — ogromna brana, izvor velike moći, jezero/rijeka. *Aspects:* lake, river, forgotten lands, civilized lands, temperate, mountainous. *Inhabitants:* humanoids, dwarves, constructs, monstrous humanoids, water dwellers.
12. **The Forgotten Library** (str. 50) — monolitni keep nad lavirintskom bibliotekom. *Aspects:* mountainous, temperate, desert, isolated, civilized. *Inhabitants:* humanoids, arcane constructs, demons, devils, undead, cultists. (Reskin: Morgrave Library / City Archive.)
13. **Castle of the Mad King** (str. 54) — ludilo skriveno iza fasade kraljevstva. *Aspects:* temperate, civilized outskirts, city. *Inhabitants:* cultists, demons, devils, monstrous humanoids, undead.
14. **The Obsidian Enclave** (str. 58) — mjesto "mračne nauke". *Aspects:* underground, unnatural, otherworldly. *Inhabitants:* subterranean creatures, evil wizards and sages, aberrations, demons, devils, constructs, liches. (Reskin: Citadel of the Closed Circle / Khyber's Gate.)
15. **Earthmote of the Elemental Lords** (str. 62) — leteće ostrvo/citadela; raspad saveza elementalnih lordova. *Aspects:* floating island, otherworldly citadel. *Inhabitants:* elementals, wizards, evil aerial beings, dragons. (Reskin: Skyway / manifest zone leta.)
16. **The Theater of the Mind** (str. 65) — pozorište iluzija sa zakopanim tajnama i aristokratijom. *Aspects:* illusion magic, deep chambers, aristocracy, buried secrets, grand theatrics. *Inhabitants:* undead, demons, devils, magical beings, constructs, magical cults, players and performers. (Reskin: Sharn Opera House / Menthis teatri / Phiarlan shadow.)
17. **The Primeval Rock** (str. 69) — meteorski krater star milion godina, primitivne zemlje. *Aspects:* desert crater, hidden and isolated, primitive lands. *Inhabitants:* primitive tribesfolk, intelligent beasts, lycanthropes, goblinoids, evil humanoids.
18. **The Blighted Evertree** (str. 73) — drevno iskvareno drvo, "stub neživota", trula šuma. *Aspects:* ancient corrupted tree, decaying forest, pillar of unlife. *Inhabitants:* corrupted natural beasts, evil humanoids, undead, demons, devils, otherworldly beasts.
19. **Stormwatch Tower** (str. 78) — ogroman airship nekako utisnut u čvrstu stijenu planinske doline. *Aspects:* mountainous valley, isolated mountains, rock canyons. *Inhabitants:* evil humanoids, elemental beings, underdark denizens, undead, monstrous humanoids. (Reskin: House Lyrandar airship / Sharn dock lore.)
20. **The Infernal Machine** (str. 83) — konstrukt sa jednom svrhom: uništiti civilizacije; veći od mnogih utvrda. *Aspects:* wastelands, isolated areas, abyssal or infernal planes. *Inhabitants:* demons, devils, cultists, evil humanoids, undead, giants, constructs, evil gods. (Uporedi: Volixus war machine iz RotLDM primjera; reskin: Cannith creation forge / Lords of Dust.)

## Napomena
Ovo su sirovi blokovi za reskin, ne Sharn sadržaj. Puni opisi (read-aloud, area-by-area aspekti, connectors, design notes) su u batchevima fl_batch_01/02. Kolona "Reskin:" su moji prijedlozi mostova ka Sharnu, ne iz knjige.
