    Sažetak 06 — Chapter 6: Heroes and Magic¶

    Mehaničko poglavlje: nove opcije za likove koji žive u Sharnu. Featovi, oprema, droge, usluge, prestige classes, spellovi i magični predmeti. Mnogo toga je vezano za manifest zone (Syrania) i radi punom snagom samo u gradu. Mehanički termini ostaju na engleskom.

    Feats (str. 157-158)¶

    • Filth Eater [General]: odrastao u kanalizaciji; +4 na saves protiv disease i ingested poisons, a kod neuspjeha ability damage smanjen za 1 (min 1).
    • Improved Flight Item [Item Creation]: (prereq Craft Wondrous Item, Knowledge planes 6) magični predmet leta u Sharnu daje +50% fly speed i dvije dodatne dnevne upotrebe (ili kontinuirano); prereq za jedinstvene Sharn predmete; construct sa fly speed dobija Manifest Flight; artificer može uzeti kao bonus feat.
    • Manifest Flight [General]: (prereq fly speed, Knowledge planes 4) u manifest zone fly speed +50%, maneuverability +1 korak.
    • Manifest Leap [General]: (prereq Knowledge planes 3, Jump 6) u zone +5 Jump, fall damage samo 1d4/10 ft; sa slow fall, +10 ft bez štete.
    • Swarm's Embrace [General]: (prereq Child of Winter) imun na distraction efekat swarmova, DR 6/— protiv swarm napada.

    Equipment (str. 158-161)¶

    Alchemical Substances (Craft alchemy): Aelia's Tonic (5 gp, -2/-4 na saves protiv ingested poison, pojačava darkeye); Darkeye (50 gp, ingested poison DC 12, paraliza + Con damage; daje vizije i +1 caster level za nekromanciju, +5 za speak with dead; služi se u Nightshade, Shae Lias); Heartflow (15 gp, afrodizijak, -3 na saves protiv charm/suggestion i Sense Motive; služi se u Firelight). Goods: Clebdecher Glamerweave (base 500 gp + 2× cijena outfita, +2 Diplomacy, status); House Sivis Notarized Document blank (2 gp, kontrabanda — za falsifikate); Potion Bracer (50 gp, 3 napitka hands-free, ali ranjiv na sunder, 5% arcane spell failure); Resilient Document (1 gp, hardness 3, otporan na vodu/vatru; resilient book +100 gp). Drugs: adikcija radi kao bolest (Fort save, ability damage). Tabela ratinga (Negligible do Extreme). Drug raste rating za korak svaka 2 mjeseca. Lesser/restoration ublažava damage, remove disease prekida adikciju, greater restoration/heal potpuno liječi.

    • Absentia (DC 16 Will, 200 gp, magična, Inspired prave, Boromar prodaju): koma 1h, percipira tuđa čula (kao scrying, nasumična meta unutar 1 milje). [DM] Skrivena nuspojava: Quori duh može posjesti tijelo bez obzira na Cha/volju. Ilegalna.
    • Dragon's Blood (Daask jedini izvor, hags Droaama prave, tajni sastojak, ilegalna): pojačava sorcerer/dragonmark moći (aberrant marks = least). Jedan elixir dnevno; svaka upotreba može voditi overdose (Will save). Black (DC 15, 240 gp, Medium addiction, Enlarge+Extend efekt, 1d4 Cha damage); Blue (DC 18, 750 gp, High, ne troši slot/upotrebu, 1d4+1 Cha); Red (DC 20, 1.200 gp, Extreme, Maximize ili +3 caster level, 1d6+1 Cha).
    • Dreamlily (DC 12, 1 gp, High addiction, opijat iz Sarlone, ilegalan): 8h potpunog mira, radi normalno na 0 do -4 hp, imun na strah; secondary 1d4 Wis damage; samo jedna akcija po rundi; addikt -2 na sve Will saves. Services and Spellcasting: barrister (1 gp/dan ili više po vještini); House Kundarak arcane seal (150 gp, gloves of the locksmith); House Sivis mark of the living parchment (50 gp).

    Prestige Classes (str. 162-167)¶

    • Cannith Wand Adept (d8; prereq Spellcraft 6, Use Magic Device 8, Craft Wand, Wand Mastery, Favored in House Cannith ili Least Mark of Making): majstor wandova. Moći: Identify Wand, Wand Focus (+class level na DC/caster level wanda, +1 attack), Wand Quick Draw, Dual Wand Use, Siphon Charge (troši charges kao action point), Improved Dragonmark.
    • Citadel Elite (d8; prereq region Breland, any lawful, BAB +7, Diplomacy 3, Gather Info 3): elitni agent krune. Moći: Citadel Training (+2 na 4 socijalne vještine), Additional Action Points, Combat Sense (defense pa attack), bonus feats, Diplomatic Protection (signet, zaštita od zakona u Brelandu/susjedima), Royal Contact, Disciplined Mind (imun na fear, +Wis na Fort), Focused Smite.
    • Sharn Skymage (d8; prereq Knowledge planes 4, Spellcraft 8, any metamagic feat, sposobnost leta spellom/prirodno — ne magičnim predmetom): majstor leta u manifest zone. Moći: Natural Flyer, Improved Flight (+class level caster level za flight spellove), Flyby Attack, Extend Flight, Improved Maneuverability, Tenacious Flight, Quicken Flight, Fast Flight (+10 ft), Reflexive Flight (cast flight na sebe kao free action dok pada). Spellcasting +1 level na 2. i 4. (Napomena iz uvoda: skymage i Manifest featovi su korisni samo u Sharnu.)
    • Other Prestige Classes for Sharn: lista PrC-jeva iz drugih suplementa pogodnih za Sharn (Complete Adventurer, Complete Arcane, Complete Divine, Complete Warrior, Expanded Psionics Handbook, Miniatures Handbook, Races of Destiny, Races of Stone) — npr. spymaster, shadow mind, vigilante, urban soul...
    • Sidebar The Urban Adept: ~2.000 adepta u Sharnu su "urban adepts" (ista progresija, jedinstvena spell lista) — rade kao healeri u Jorasco, gatari, alhemičari. Data je puna spell lista (0-5 level).

    Magic (str. 168-172)¶

    Primarni efekt manifest zone (Syrania): pojačava spellove/predmete levitacije i leta. Bez toga tornjevi bi se srušili, a Skyway pao.

    Spells¶

    • Arcane Seal (Abjuration; Magewright 3, sorc/wiz 3): pečati vrata/škrinju/portal + mistični alarm. Focus: platinum key (50 gp), nositelj ključa slobodno prolazi i dobija mentalni alert ako se otvori. +10 na DC za provalu; knock suzbije 10 min; dispel magic uništi (ali alert i dalje ide). Rogue/artificer detektuje Search DC 28.
    • Force Hammer (Evocation [Force]; sorc/wiz 2): ray, 1d4 nonlethal/level (max 10d4), Fort partial ili dazed 1 rundu. Bez efekta na neživo.
    • Shockwave (Evocation [Force]; sorc/wiz 3): 20-ft burst, 1d4 nonlethal/caster level (max 10d4), dazed 1 rundu (Fort half + negira daze). Alternativa fireballu na ulici. Material: mali kristal.

    Magic Items¶

    Greater Dragonshard Focus Items: Gloves of the Locksmith (arcane seal 3/dan, 10.000 gp, Mark of Warding); Pen of the Living Parchment (trajne arcane marks na živo meso, za žigosanje zločinaca, 22.500 gp, Mark of Scribing); Sentinel's Shield (quality, protection from arrows 3/dan, +4.200 gp, Mark of Sentinel). Wondrous Items: Arcane Key (rogue otvara magične brave/alarme, ilegalan u Sharnu, 500 gp); Eye of Aureon (trajni zone of truth DC 20 u 20 ft, sudnice, 102.000 gp); Feather Fall Talisman (jednokratni feather fall, 50 gp); Flying Buttress (drži gornje nivoe tornja u zraku, 48.000 gp); Lift (magični lift, 24.000 gp); Mask of the Misplaced Aura (lažni identitet protiv divinacije, koriste rakshase/doppelgangeri, 77.000 gp); Pendant of Mystic Warning (stalni detect magic 30 ft, samo arcane caster, 8.000 gp; koriste perceptori u sudu); Powder of Silent Passage (suzbija alarm/arcane seal, ilegalan, 300 gp); Shiftweave (5 outfita u jednoj odjeći, free action mijenjanje, 500 gp); Soarsled (kristalni disk, 90 ft fly u Sharnu, 38.000 gp); Voluminous Vault (sef sa extradimensional prostorom + arcane seal, steel 17.500 / adamantine 27.500 gp); Watch Lamp (globus svjetla, 1.500 gp); Wizard's Manacles (adamantine, Escape Artist/Str DC 40, arcane seal, 3.250 gp). Artifacts: The Cauldron of Boldrei — drevni bakarni kotao u ruševinama Godsgate (Fallen), nepomičan; svako jutro se napuni sivom kašom koja hrani 60 ljudi (CL 20, immobile).

    Nesigurno / za provjeru¶

    • Sve cijene, DC-ovi i mehanike prepisani tačno iz tabela. Puni statblokovi prestige classes i predmeta su u batchu; ovdje su sažeti opisi.
