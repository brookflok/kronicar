    Glosar — Sharn: City of Towers¶

    Imenovani pojmovi iz knjige, na engleskom, sa kratkim opisom. Uz svaki ide gdje se pojavljuje (batch). Organizovano po kategorijama.

    Quarters, wards i glavne oblasti¶

    • Central Plateau — srce grada; vlada, finansije, dragonmarked enklave. (b02)
    • Cliffside — lučki ward na liticama uz Dagger River. (b02)
    • Dura — najveći i najstariji quarter, najsiromašniji. (b02)
    • Menthis Plateau — zabava i Morgrave University. (b02)
    • Northedge — najtiši, rezidentni quarter. (b02)
    • Skyway — lebdeći ward najbogatijih. (b02)
    • Tavick's Landing — kapija grada (rail i Old Road). (b02)
    • The Cogs — podzemna industrija (Ashblack, Blackbones, Khyber's Gate). (b02)
    • City of the Dead — kripte na liticama, van manifest zone. (b02)
    • The Depths / UnderSharn — ruševine starih gradova između Lower-City i Cogs. (b02)
    • The Sewers — kanalizacija ispod Lower-City, sewer clans. (b02)

    Ključni districti (izbor)¶

    • Highest Towers — civic district, Council Hall, City Archive (Upper Central). (b02)
    • Korranath / Korran-Thiven — finance districti; Kundarak Bank, hram Kol Korran (Upper Central). (b02)
    • Dragon Towers — guildhall district, dragonmarked enklave (Middle Central). (b02)
    • Ambassador Towers — embassy district + The Citadel/zatvor (Middle Central). (b02)
    • Sovereign Towers — temple district; Pavilion of the Host, Cathedral of the Cleansing Flame (Middle Central). (b02)
    • Clifftop — adventurer's quarter (Upper Dura). (b02)
    • The Bazaar — najveća pijaca (Middle Dura). (b02)
    • Callestan / Fallen / Malleon's Gate — opasni districti Lower Dure. (b02)
    • University District — Morgrave University (Upper Menthis). (b02)
    • Den'iyas — gnome četvrt "Little Zilargo" (Upper Menthis). (b02)
    • Everbright — magic district (Middle Menthis). (b02)
    • Little Plains — halfling četvrt, Boromar HQ (Middle Menthis). (b02)
    • Torchfire / Smoky Towers — teatar districti (Menthis). (b02)
    • Firelight / Dragoneyes / Sharn's Welcome — red light districti. (b01/b02)
    • Shae Lias — elf četvrt (Upper Northedge). (b02)
    • Holdfast / Highhold — dwarf četvrti (Northedge / Dura). (b02)
    • Deathsgate — adventurer's quarter (Middle Tavick's Landing). (b02)
    • Graywall — karrnska četvrt, Blood of Vol (Middle Tavick's Landing). (b02)
    • High Walls — izbjeglički slum (Lower Tavick's Landing). (b02)
    • Khyber's Gate — undercity, Daask HQ (Upper Cogs). (b02)
    • Overlook — kalashtar/Adaran četvrt (Upper Dura). (b02)
