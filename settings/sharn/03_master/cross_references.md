# Unakrsne veze — Sharn: City of Towers

Veze između organizacija, ličnosti, lokacija i događaja. Pomaže da se brzo vidi ko je s kim povezan i gdje stvari vode. [DM] sadržaj.

## Velike skrivene niti (tko zapravo povlači konce)
- **Dreaming Dark (Inspired, iz Dal Quora):** dominator **Tirashana** → mind seeds **Bestan ir'Tonn** (councilor, Upper Tavick's) i **Maira ir'Talan** (Lady Warden, Blackened Book) → kontroliše i cijelu Sharn enklavu **House Deneith** (psionika, "ascension"/quori posjedovanje). Veza i sa drogom **absentia** (omogućava quori posjedovanje) i agentom **Hamatash** (Dar San, Precarious). Ambasada **Riedra** (Jorasesh) sluša Dreaming Dark agente.
- **Lords of Dust (rakshase):** **Zathara** i **Nethatar** → glume ambasadora "Helais ir'Lantar" (Aundair); baza u Workshop of Tyrial Dashar (Khyber's Gate); sarađuju sa Archierophant **Ythana Morr** (Cathedral of the Cleansing Flame). Prijetnja **Luca Syari** (eladrin).
- **Aurum:** **Kalphan Riak** (Platinum Concord, Korranath) — najviši u Sharnu; **Borian Haldorak** (councilor Upper Dura, Gold Concord); **Lain of Clan Soldorak** (Mror konzul); **Haras Kant** (Crystal Bridge); **Billan Tosh** (Twelve Pillars clerk). Pregovaraju sa Boromarima preko Borian↔Ilyra.
- **Sora Katra** (vještica Droaama) → vodi **Daask** preko **Cavallah** (hag eye/sending); misterija droge **dragon's blood**.

## Rat kriminalnih frakcija
- **Boromar Clan** (Saidan Boromar, Little Plains) ⚔ **Daask** (Cavallah, Khyber's Gate) — aktivni rat, trese Lower Dura, Callestan, Cogsgate, Oldkeep.
- **Boromar** ↔ **House Tarkanan** (primirje: Tarkanan ne dira Boromare; Boromar koristi Tarkanan za ubistva).
- **Boromar** ↔ **The Tyrants** (300-godišnje primirje, različite arene).
- **Boromar** veze: councilori **Ilyra Boromar, Bestan ir'Tonn, Caskar Halavik, Thurik Davandi**; Watch komandant **Lian Halamar**; Royal Guard kapetan **Toras Cant**; House Jorasco (preko žene **Mala Boromar d'Jorasco**); councilorka **Savia Potellas** plaća tribut.
- **Tyrants** kontrolišu identitet **"Kilk"** (councilor) i bordele; **Velvet's, Chance, Cracked Mirror, Shifting Hall** (Dragoneyes); doppelganger **Zaz** (Callestan).

## Dragonmarked houses → lokacije i ljudi
- **House Cannith** → **Merrix d'Cannith** → Cannith Enclave (Dragon Towers) + **Forgehold** i tajni **creation forge** (Ashblack); **Tarya d'Cannith** (dragon's blood istraga); **Darkwood Watch** (Menya). Veza sa **Tyrants/Traveler** (zajednička vjera).
- **House Deneith** → kompromitovan od Dreaming Dark (**Sadran d'Deneith**); Blademark, Defender's Guild, Sentinel Marshals; enklave Dragon Towers + Copper Arch (psionika); jaki u Upper Tavick's Landing.
- **House Kundarak** → **Daphanë** → Kundarak Bank (Korranath), the Vaults, sigurna skladišta (Precarious, Cogsgate); pregovara o rušenju monarhije sa **Ruken ir'Clarn** i **Hass ir'Tain**.
- **House Tharashk** → inquisitivi i vodiči za Xen'drik; veza sa Droaam; **Globe Information Agency, Information Acquisition, Karr'Aashta's, Thuranne d'Velderan**.
- **House Sivis** → komunikacija/notarizacija; **Solirion** (Dragon Towers); falsifikati blank dokumenata (kontrabanda) → veza sa forgerima i Tyrants.
- **House Jorasco** (healing) ↔ Boromar; **House Ghallanda** (hospitality) → Ghallanda Hall, secure shelters; **House Vadalis** → gargoyle kuriri, pegasi (Pegasus Spire); **House Lyrandar** → airship/weather; **House Orien** → lightning rail (Terminus); **House Phiarlan** (shadow/spies) → **Athania, Tyasha**.

## Zavjera o monarhiji
- **Lord Ruken/Duken ir'Clarn** (Parlament) + **Hass ir'Tain** (Skyway/Parlament) + councilorka **Evix ir'Marasha** (Skyway) + **House Kundarak** (ljuti zbog neplaćenih ratnih zajmova) → plan ukidanja brelishke monarhije nakon smrti kralja **Boranel**. Suprotno: **King's Citadel** lovi prijetnje kruni.

## Religija → lokacije → tajne
- **Cathedral of the Cleansing Flame** (Silver Flame, Sovereign Towers) → korumpirana **Ythana Morr** ↔ rakshasa Zathara. Iskreni Silver Flame: **Mazin Tana** (Coldflame Keep), **Kaira Faine** (Shrine of Fathen). Templari smiju loviti "inhuman darkness".
- **The Keeper** (Dark Six) → shrine-ovi: Vundry/Gellis Candar (Bazaar), Cracked Mirror (Callestan); lich **Gath** (City of the Dead).
- **Blood of Vol** → **House of Hazal** (Graywall, **Lan Hazal**) → snabdijeva vampira **Jesel Tarra'az** (Gray House) → **Order of the Emerald Claw**.
- **Undying Court** → **Gates of Passage / Mayne Jhaelian** (Shae Lias) ↔ konzul Aerenala **Taelira**.
- **Radiant Idol Zotharr** → kult **Kielsten Marquan** (Marquan's Rooms, Boldrei's Hearth).

## Lokacije → resursi za party
- **Morgrave University** (University District) → biblioteka (+bonusi), mudraci, ekspedicije Xen'drik, **Flamewind** (proročanstva), Dezina Museum; ali **Dala Arand** špijun Emerald Claw, **Karg** (ogre učenjak iz Droaama).
- **Clifftop** (Upper Dura) → Clifftop Guild, Esoteric Order of Aureon, **The Augury/Kestia**, Drunken Dragon (unajmiti avanturiste), House Tharashk/Sivis.
- **Deathsgate** (Middle Tavick's) → Deathsgate Guild, Guild of Starlight and Shadow, magic/spellcasting; ali skriveni shrine Mockery, **Karr'Aashta** (Dragon Below).
- **The Bazaar** (Middle Dura) → sve, uklj. fence **Vundry**; Boromar vs Daask tenzija.
- **Everbright** (Middle Menthis) → najbolji magic item dileri i casteri 10+.

## Događaji → posljedice (historija i tekuće)
- **War of the Mark** → razoren stari grad → ruševine **UnderSharn/Depths**, kletva → **feral spirits** (Tarkananovi ratnici); ime **House Tarkanan** (po Halas Tarkanan); **Iron Guardian** statua (Skysedge Park).
- **Glass Tower** pao (918 YK) → district **Fallen** (Lower Dura), **the Ravers**, izgubljeno blago; praznik **Crystalfall**.
- **Day of Mourning** (994 YK) → uništenje **Cyre** → izbjeglice u **High Walls**; **New Cyre**; kult Dragon Below (**Chyrassk**) regrutuje traumirane Cyrane; praznik **Day of Mourning**.
- **The Last War** → **warforged** u Cogs (skoro robovi), **Merrix** ih štiti; **Redcloak Battalion** (ratni veterani); izbjeglice; **Treaty of Thronehold** (warforged dobili prava).
- **Day of Ashes** (943 YK) → poraz **Lorda Kellas ir'Moran**, njegova mržnja elfova/Cyrana.

## Sportovi/događaji → lokacije
- **Race of Eight Winds** → počinje/završava u **Hareth's Folly** (Dura); pegasi iz **Pegasus Spire**.
- **Hrazhak** → arene **Stone Trees** (Cornerstone) i Cornerstone; shifteri (Lower Northedge, Stoneyard).
- **Skyblades** → **Hollow Tower** (Hareth's Folly), Cornerstone, Hareth's Folly.
- **Six Stones** → ilegalno, **Daask**, Dura Cogs (medusa Karyale, cockatrices).
- **The Burning Ring** → ilegalna borba, **Hoas Junter**/Daask (Firelight).
- **Tain Gala** → **Tain Manor** (Skyway), definiše "The Sixty".

## Brze "ako PC uradi X" niti
- Naljutiš člana **Esoteric Order** → cijene spellova ti se dupliraju.
- Ubiješ Aurum mastermind → drugi član te tuži za "ubistvo" (sudski hook).
- Nosiš aberrant mark → **House Tarkanan** te vrbuje; **Silaena Cazal** (Watch) ti potajno pomaže.
- Tražiš novi identitet/skrivanje → **Tyrants** (Shifting Hall, Dragoneyes).
- Tražiš dragon's blood → **Daask** (Wounded Dragon, Cask, Khyber's Gate).
- Trebaš castera 7+ levela ili raise dead → rijetki imenovani (Ythana Morr, Phthaso Mogan, Mayne Jhaelian, Lan Hazal) — traže uslugu, ne zlato.
